About the OECD

Established in 1961, the aim of the Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) has always been to promote policies that improve the economic and social well-being of people around the world by using international forums in which governments can work together to seek viable solutions to common problems that overwhelming to the world and their countries.
